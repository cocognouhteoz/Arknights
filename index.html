Operators — Choose your assistant

As Arknights is a tower defense game, Operators essentially act as the "towers"; the player places Operators in locations around the battlefield to prevent enemies from reaching the objective.
Operators come in six rarities, denoted by a number of stars from 1 to 6.

Rhodes Island

Rhodes Island is a company situated near the city of Chernobog. Their goal is to find a cure for infected individuals with the plague virus. Rhodes Island also has a goal of restoring peace and order to Chernobog, Rhodes Island also contracts with several other military groups like Blacksteel.

Rhine lab

The Rhine Lab, or R.L. for short, is an organization in the world of Arknights. They are the strongest and also the protectors of the world but tend to be more violent

Penguin Logistics

Penguin Logistics is a logistics company that works in close liaison with Rhodes Island. As the essential supply line for a variety of necessities, Penguin Logistics selects only the most capable operators and has an almost endless amount of supplies that it offers to alliances as well as maintaining itself.

Great Lungmen

The Great Lungmen, often simply referred to as Lungmen, is a country in the world of Arknights.Great Lungmen is a parallel to Hong Kong, which can be seen from it being technically part of Yen but maintains a greater autonomy and having greater foreign influences.

Many times I’ve accidentally placed a character in a wrong spot or wrong direction with no way to fix it without withdrawing it and having to wait 1 minute to redeploy. And the friends system. It’s impossible to find friends in this game which is weird cause there is a function that requires friends (clues). If there could be a confirmation put in place when deploying and a random friend search function this would be 10/10 👍🏻

As a person who normally plays on PC and isn’t very committed to playing mobile games I can say this: This is the only mobile game I have not quit after a few days of playing. I enjoy the gameplay, missions, characters, and story. The game is easy to learn and has lots of content for you at the start. But later on the missions cost too much sanity (sanity = energy) with a low sanity limit and you don’t regen it fast. But overall the gameplay is solid, with minor flaws in sanity cap/regen. Overall I can’t force you to play but I can recommend trying this game with a rating of 10/10.
